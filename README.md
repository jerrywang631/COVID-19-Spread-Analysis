# Team & Acknowledgements

This project was a collaborative effort by Zhenming Liu, Ziheng Wang, Jialun Wei, and Ruocheng Wen for the CSC111 course project at UTSG. 

Together, our team successfully:
*  Designed and implemented a custom 6-level `Tree` data structure, collecting datasets from the official site of Canada and US to sort and store them into a large-scale COVID-19 datasets.
*  Developed backend computation logic to calculate regional case densities and average growth rates to directly show the extent of the pandemic in the specific location and duration.
*  Integrated Plotly and Pandas libarary to build an interactive CLI tool and generate geographic heatmaps to better visuialize the effect of the pandemic in Canada and US.
